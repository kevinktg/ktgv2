### Task V4 — Decision gate (blocking): whatever makes the handoff read

**Goal:** Visible, scroll-linked wipe between sections — **not** a fixed recipe. `100vh` / `+=100vh` in earlier discussion were **examples** of viewport-sized scroll span, not mandatory values.

**Pick the smallest change that works** (examples, not requirements):

- Taller/shorter trigger band (`vh`, `clamp`, `min()` — whatever matches design).
- Or adjust ScrollTrigger `start` / `end` (e.g. relative `+=` scroll distance) so scrub progress matches perception.
- Or **C:** neither structural change — only stacking/docs — if that alone fixes it.

**Resume:** Describe intent (“more scroll per wipe”, “taller band”, etc.) or pick a concrete tweak after V1–V3.
